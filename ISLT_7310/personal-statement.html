My personal belief in God has profoundly influenced my approach to life and work, guiding me to seek wisdom and direction through the Bible. Viewing the Scriptures as a moral compass, I strive to align my actions and decisions with its teachings. This spiritual foundation is complemented by listening to Christian music, which helps me maintain a positive attitude, especially during challenging jobs. Despite working overnights, I make it a point to attend church in Columbia, recognizing the significance of communal worship and its impact on my work ethic. From a young age, my involvement with the Christian community has shaped my perspective, reinforcing values of dedication and integrity that I carry into my professional life.



Achieving success at a young age has fueled my desire to extend similar opportunities to others. My strong work ethic and commitment to my goals were evident during high school, where I balanced work with dual-credit classes, demonstrating my ability to manage multiple responsibilities. A notable achievement was purchasing a brand-new car with a substantial down payment, which I accomplished without a cosigner. This financial independence and accomplishment are a testament to my drive and determination. In my current role, I leverage these experiences to help others, training new employees at Walmart and in a hospital setting, aiming to provide them with the guidance and support necessary for their growth and success.



The drive to excel and support others in their professional journey is a cornerstone of my approach to work. My ability to navigate and balance full-time work with full-time studies showcases my dedication and strong work ethic. By fostering an environment where individuals are empowered to reach their potential, I contribute not only to my personal success but also to the success of those around me. This commitment to excellence and mentorship reflects my broader goal of enhancing organizational performance and fostering a collaborative, high-achieving workplace.



Overall, I believe I would be an asset to any organization, bringing a wealth of experience, a robust work ethic, and a commitment to personal and professional growth. My background, characterized by successful achievements and a dedication to supporting others, equips me to make meaningful contributions to your team. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experiences align with your organization's needs and how I can contribute to its continued success.
